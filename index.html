About me

I’m Chika Egulemu, a passionate and highly driven creative with a deep love for storytelling through design. With over six years of experience in branding, visual communication, and motion design, I specialize in bringing ideas to life through bold visuals and clean, intentional aesthetics. My creative journey started with a certification from Ambidextrous Design HUB and has since grown into a purpose-led career that connects people, builds identity, and drives engagement across various platforms. Beyond design, I’m inspired by culture, innovation, and community. I believe in using creativity as a tool to educate, inspire, and uplift. I’ve worked with diverse clients—from startups to public sector bodies—crafting logos, campaigns, and visual systems that speak authentically to audiences. I value impact, clarity, and collaboration, and I’m always learning and pushing boundaries to better serve those I work with. Whether I’m sketching new concepts, animating motion graphics, or leading a rebrand, I bring intention, empathy, and excellence to the table. Outside of work, I’m a lifelong learner, a lover of music, and a voice for empowering young talents. I’m building a creative legacy, one design at a time.
